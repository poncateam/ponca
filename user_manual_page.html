Fitting

Nicolas Mellado, Thibault Lejemble

This module provides efficient methods for the fitting and analysis of point-clouds in arbitrary dimensions. Sounds a bit abstract, and indeed, it is templated in every corner of the code.

It's worth the effort though, as it may be used for varied purposes such as curvature computation, surface variation computation, surface reconstruction, scale-space analysis, image processing, sketch vectorization and much more!

The good news is that the module is quite easy to use and extensible thanks to its programming interface that allows you to customize it to your needs.
