template<typename Index , typename Scalar , int DIM>
using Ponca::KdTreeDefaultNode< Index, Scalar, DIM >::InnerType = KdTreeDefaultInnerNode<Index, Scalar, DIM>

The type of the inner member.

Must provide a split_value member of type Scalar, a first_child_id member and a dim member.

Must also provide DIM_BITS and INDEX_BITS compile-time constants specifying the number of bits used to store dim and first_child_id respecitvely. These constants are used to determine the maximum number of points the kd-tree can store.
